➕ When compared with other methodologies, Waterfall focuses most on a clear, defined set of steps. ➖ The Waterfall model entirely depends on the sequential steps which make the team move forward. The Waterfall methodology is a traditional approach which leaves almost no room for revisions or changes after completion of each step.
